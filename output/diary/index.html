Rōmaji Diary

Takuboku kept a diary from 1902 to 1912. The portion for the year 1909, which he wrote in romanized Japanese so that his wife could not read it, is regarded as a classic in the diary genre. Parts of the latter were published in 1909 as Rōmaji nikki. It was not published in full until 1977. In this diary, Takuboku recorded with overpowering honesty his complex emotional and intellectual life.
